## Pricing & Tracks

**Q: How much does Future Proof Bootcamp cost?**
Three tracks, all $699 and under per month, and every tier includes a project completion guarantee:
- POD — $399/month — 4-5 students per session
- POD+ — $499/month — 2-3 students per session
- PREMIUM — $699/month — 1:1 with Coach Indy

**Q: What's the difference between the tracks?**
Same core curriculum and the same project completion guarantee across all three. The difference is
group size and how much 1:1 time your kid gets with Coach Indy — POD is small-group, POD+ is smaller,
PREMIUM is entirely 1:1.

**Q: Is there a discount if I do the program with my kid?**
Yes — parents who go through the course alongside their student get $100/month off.

**Q: How do I pay?**
For the first cohort, payment is by check (card/Stripe payments are coming later, not yet live).

## The Guarantee

**Q: What's the "project completion guarantee"?**
It's a real contract, not a marketing phrase — every tier guarantees your kid finishes something real
they can show the world, not just watch videos or do busywork.

## Parent Role

**Q: What's expected of parents during the course?**
Emotional support only. Parents sign a Declaration committing to NOT do the work, manage the project, or
"foreman" behind the scenes — this course is specifically designed around fixing that dynamic. Regular
3-way check-ins (student + parent + Coach Indy) are scheduled to keep everyone aligned without parents
taking over.

## What Students Need / Costs Beyond Tuition

**Q: Does my kid need to already know how to code?**
No. Coach Indy went from zero coding experience to deploying two live AI-powered businesses himself —
the course is built to take a real beginner to a real, deployed project.

**Q: What do we need before Day 1?**
A "Student Backpack," sent right after enrollment: a new professional Gmail and Discord account, roughly
$300 loaded onto a Wise virtual card (the student buys their own project supplies — procurement is part
of the curriculum), an AI subscription (Claude or ChatGPT, ~$20/month), and a few physical supplies like
an engineering composition book. Full manifest and links provided at enrollment.
